about us

a wee bit of ireland

We're a comfy Irish pub located near McCoy Stadium in Pawtucket, RI. Established in 2007, we pride ourselves on creating an authentic Irish Pub experience with a home away from home feeling.

We're a comfy Irish pub located near McCoy Stadium in Pawtucket, RI. Established in 2007, we pride ourselves on creating an authentic Irish Pub experience with a home away from home feeling. We welcome people from all walks of life to enjoy a friendly, relaxing environment after work and on the weekends. We are involved in many local Irish events, including but not limited to the Pawtucket Saint Patrick's Day Parade, fundraisers, and Toys for Tots. We love sharing the history we grew up and bringing a taste of Ireland to the city of Pawtucket.
